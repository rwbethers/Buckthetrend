FAQ

Everything you're wondering about
working with Buckhorn.

What exactly is a fractional CFO?+
A fractional CFO is a seasoned financial executive who works with your company part-time or on a contract basis. You get all the strategic expertise of a Fortune 500 CFO — cash flow management, financial forecasting, bank relationships — without paying a $180,000+ annual salary. Buckhorn pricing is custom-built around your business size and needs.
How is Buckhorn different from my accountant or bookkeeper?+
Your accountant looks backward — they file taxes and record history. Buckhorn looks forward. We use your financial data to build strategies, forecast cash flow, identify growth opportunities, and help you make smarter decisions before problems arise. Most clients use Buckhorn alongside their existing accountant.
What size companies do you typically work with?+
We primarily work with Utah small and mid-size businesses generating between $500K and $10M in annual revenue. If you're too small for a full-time CFO but big enough to need real financial strategy, Buckhorn is built for you.
What happens during the free 15-minute demo?+
We'll spend 15 minutes learning about your business, identifying your biggest financial pain points, and showing you exactly how Buckhorn would approach your situation. There's zero pressure and zero obligation. If it's not a fit, we'll tell you and point you in the right direction.
How quickly will I see results?+
Most clients identify their first financial inefficiency within the first 30 days. Meaningful improvements in cash flow visibility, reporting accuracy, and strategic clarity typically occur within 60–90 days.
Do I have to commit to a long-term contract?+
We structure engagements based on your needs. Some clients prefer month-to-month flexibility, while others benefit from longer commitments that allow deeper strategic work. We'll discuss the right structure for your business during your free demo.
